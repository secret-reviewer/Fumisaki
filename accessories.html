Rider Log Entry Touring must have accessories

GPS system, charging for your phone/camera, mounting points

The Accessories

Tested several Car GPS system (standalone version) but realised that waterproof will be a main issue, decidede to go with a properly motorcycle standalone GPS system (Tomtom 550) as I'm worried that Thailand might not be able to connect to network fast enough or I might be up in the mountains with poor internet. Set up a few mounting points for my insta360 as well (ended up didn't use) Regretted not fixing a phone charger and thought power bank is sufficient, I was wrong. A must to get charging system on your motorcycle.

Key Takeaways

Wasted a ton of money on car GPS, don't follow my steps, get one good GPS system and settled for it Be it carplay or standalone, a must to get charging system on bike, preferable wireless charging so that you don't need to deal with wires while on bike. Mounting points, choose one to 2 max, you can't be bothered much while you are on the road while food and drinks is your main concern + miss exits. Charger- my iPhone battery went from 100% to 20% due to heat + taking tons of photos and videos while on the road and power bank doesn't charge fast enough, at times, need to rely back on phone GPS system as Tomtom maps is outdated at some place.
