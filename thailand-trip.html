Hatyai Convoy 2012 Recap

Road Trip: SG to Hatyai 🇹🇭

Reliving the golden days of the 2012 Convoy

"It wasn't just a ride; it was a big deal. The wind in our faces, the sound of the engines, and the bond of the brotherhood."

📍 Trip Schedule

  • Day 1: The Long Haul (3:15 AM Move off). Arrival in Hatyai by late afternoon.
  • Day 2: Bike TLC & Chill. Massage, Tuk-Tuks, and local food.
  • Day 3: Ride to Ko Yo, Songkhla for breathtaking views.
  • Day 4: The Rainy Journey Home. 14 hours of grit and determination.

🛠️ Rider Readiness

Being prepared is the key to happiness on the road:

  • Mechanical: Tyre kits, spark plugs, and clutch cables are a must.
  • Safety: Proper gear only. No shorts, no half-caps.
  • Mindset: Know your bike, ride your own pace, and never stop on the roadside.

💰 The Damage (Estimated)

  • Petrol & Tolls: ~S$110
  • Hotel (3 Nights): ~S$150
  • Food & Misc: ~S$100 - S$150
  • Recommended Pocket Money: S$400 - S$500
